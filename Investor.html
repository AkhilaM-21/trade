ANNEXURE A & B – INVESTOR CHARTER & COMPLAINT DATA

The following Annexures provide a comprehensive overview of investor rights, compliance responsibilities, and transparent data on complaint handling for Research Analysts. Annexure A details the Investor Charter, while Annexure B summarizes complaint statistics and resolution trends, ensuring best-practice transparency for all stakeholders.

ANNEXURE A
Investor Charter in respect of RAs

This section describes the Investor Charter for Research Analysts. Click below to view the full details.

A. Vision and Mission Statements for Investors
Vision: Invest with knowledge & safety.
Mission: Every investor should be able to invest in right investment products based on their needs, manage and monitor them to meet their goals, access reports and enjoy financial wellness.
B. Business transacted by the Research Analyst
  • Publish research reports based on research activities
  • Provide an independent unbiased view on securities
  • Offer recommendations disclosing financial interests
  • Base recommendations on public information and known observations
  • Conduct annual audit
  • Adhere to Advertisement Code for Research Analysts
  • Maintain client interaction records
C. Services provided to investors
  • Client onboarding and KYC
  • Disclosures about business, risks, and conflicts
  • Use of Artificial Intelligence tools (if any) in research
  • Maintain confidentiality and data privacy
  • Guidance for high-risk financial products
  • Treat all clients with honesty and integrity
D. Grievance Redressal Mechanism
  • Investor can complain directly to the Research Analyst (RA)
  • Use SEBI's SCORES system (scores.sebi.gov.in) or email RAASB
  • If unsatisfied, escalate via SMARTODR or physical SEBI office
E. Rights of Investors
  • Right to privacy, information, fair treatment, and to be heard
  • Right to initial and continuing disclosure
  • Right to feedback and exit services as per agreement
  • Additional rights for vulnerable/disabled consumers
F. Responsibilities/Expectations (Do’s & Don’ts)
Do’s:
  • Deal only with SEBI registered analysts
  • Check SEBI registration number and disclosures
  • Pay through banking channels, keep receipts
  • Seek clarifications and exercise all rights granted
  • Report analysts offering assured returns
Don’ts:
  • Never provide funds to the analyst for investment
  • Don’t trust luring ads or promises of guaranteed returns
  • Never share account credentials
