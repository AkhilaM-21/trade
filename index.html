Technical Analysis

Technical analysis involves studying price charts, market patterns, and indicators to predict future price movements. By analyzing trends, support and resistance levels, and trading volumes, traders gain actionable insights for entry and exit points. It’s a key tool for making informed decisions, identifying opportunities, and managing risk in various market conditions.

Open Interest Data Analysis

Open Interest Data Analysis monitors the number of outstanding contracts in options and futures markets. By tracking changes in open interest, traders can gauge market sentiment and spot strong trends or reversals. It’s a powerful tool for identifying potential price movements, validating trading signals, and making informed decisions in fast-moving markets.
